Dataset DATA4-12 contains information about 51 states in USA. Every state is desscribed by 11 demographic and economic features:
-MORT: mortality per 100 000 residents
-INCC: income per capita in dollars,
-POV: fraction of families living below poverty line,
-EDU1: fraction of population that finished 4 years of high school education,
-EDU2: fraction of population that finished 4 years of university education,
-ALCC: alcohol consumption in gallons,
-TOBC: tobacco consumption per capita in packs,
-HEXC: healthcare expenditure per capita in dollars,
-PHYS: number of doctors per 100 000 residents,
-URB: fraction of population living in urban areas,
-AGED: fraction of population older than 65 years
